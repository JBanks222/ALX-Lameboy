INSTRUCTIONS

GAME CONTROLS

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move spaceship
  • J - Shoot lasers
  • B - Ultimate blast (when bar is full)
  • START - Start/Restart game

ULTIMATE SYSTEM

  • Fill the ultimate bar by destroying enemies
  • Each kill fills 10% of the bar
  • When full, press B for a powerful laser blast
  • Ultimate destroys all enemies in its path

SCORING

  • Destroy enemy: 50 points
  • Enemy escapes: 10 points
  • Avoid collisions to survive!

RADIO

  • Click the radio or press R
  • Plays ALX-Crazy.mp3
